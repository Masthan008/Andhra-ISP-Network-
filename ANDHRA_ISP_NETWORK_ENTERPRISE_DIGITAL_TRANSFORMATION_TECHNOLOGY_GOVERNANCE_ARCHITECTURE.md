## 1. Executive Summary & Digital Transformation Vision

The **Andhra ISP Network Master Enterprise Digital Transformation, Technology Governance & Organizational Maturity Architecture** defines the target Enterprise Operating Model, 5-Level Capability Maturity Framework, Architecture Review Board (ARB) governance standards, Technology Adoption Radar, 3-Year Transformation Roadmap, Innovation Funnel, Enterprise Decision Framework, and Institutional Knowledge Preservation systems.

Engineered to guide **Andhra ISP Network's evolution into a world-class, nationally scaled digital infrastructure enterprise**, this framework establishes transparent governance, architecture-led decision making, and operational excellence without compromising agility or innovation velocity.
