## 1. Executive Summary & Governance Vision

The **Andhra ISP Network Master Enterprise Governance, Audit, Compliance & Regulatory Excellence Architecture** defines the corporate governance operating model, 9 Governance Committees (Executive Board, ARB, Security Council, AI Council, etc.), 3-Tier Internal Control Framework (Preventive, Detective, Corrective), Immutable WORM Audit Evidence Vaults, Policy Lifecycle Management, DPDP 2023 Compliance Monitoring, and 5-Level Governance Maturity Model.

Engineered to ensure **unbroken compliance, digital trust, and organizational accountability across 26 districts of Andhra Pradesh and future national expansions**, this framework establishes policy-driven operations and continuous audit readiness.
