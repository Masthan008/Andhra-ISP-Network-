## 1. Executive Summary & Enablement Vision

The **Andhra ISP Network Master Enterprise Learning, Training, Onboarding & Organizational Enablement Architecture** defines the "Learning-by-Design" organizational vision, 14 Role-Based Curricula, Day 1 to Day 90 Onboarding milestones, 18 Specialized Learning Paths, 4-Tier Certification Lifecycle, Competency Capability Maps, and Guild-based Mentorship Networks.

Engineered to support **rapid, friction-free onboarding of engineers, operations teams, ISP partners, and government administrators across 26 districts of Andhra Pradesh and future national expansions**, this framework guarantees operational readiness, zero knowledge decay, and continuous engineering excellence.

## 5. Mentorship Guilds & Communities of Practice (CoP)

Engineering capability is sustained through 5 **Guild Communities**:
1. **Architecture Guild:** Weekly ADR reviews, design pattern sharing, and Tech Radar assessments.
2. **Frontend Guild:** GSAP animation optimizations, Canvas performance tuning, and accessibility audits.
3. **DevSecOps & SRE Guild:** Chaos engineering simulations, Kubernetes topology reviews, and post-mortem analysis.
4. **AI & Data Guild:** RAG prompt engineering, vector embedding benchmarks, and MLOps pipelines.
5. **Operations & Partner Guild:** Regional ISP onboarding SOPs, Mandal-level coverage audits, and SLA reviews.
