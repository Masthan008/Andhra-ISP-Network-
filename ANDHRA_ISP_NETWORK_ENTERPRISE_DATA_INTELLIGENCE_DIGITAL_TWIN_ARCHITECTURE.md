## 5. Data Observability & Knowledge Graph Governance

Data integrity is maintained across 5 **Observability Pillars**:
1. **Freshness:** Real-time ingestion latency monitoring for PostGIS spatial updates and ISP plan changes.
2. **Completeness:** Automated validation of Mandal polygon coverage and Provider registration data.
3. **Quality & Lineage:** End-to-end data lineage tracking from source DB schemas to executive PDF reports.
4. **Knowledge Graph Entities:** Contextual graph linking Mandals $\rightarrow$ LCOs $\rightarrow$ Bandwidth Hubs $\rightarrow$ Citizen Requests.
5. **Privacy Safeguards:** Automated K-anonymity masking enforcing DPDP 2023 compliance on analytics exports.
