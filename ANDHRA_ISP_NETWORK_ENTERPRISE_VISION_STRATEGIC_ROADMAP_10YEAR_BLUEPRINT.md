## 1. Executive Summary & Enterprise Vision Statement

The **Andhra ISP Network Master Enterprise Vision, Strategic Roadmap & 10-Year Platform Blueprint** defines the 10-Year Phased Horizon, Enterprise Capability Map across 12 strategic domains, Digital Public Infrastructure (DPI) alignment, National Expansion Framework, Responsible AI Vision, Business Sustainability Models, and Executive Scenario Planning.

Designed to transition **Andhra ISP Network from Andhra Pradesh's premier broadband discovery platform into a national Digital Public Good (DPG) and sovereign infrastructure layer**, this blueprint ensures unbroken backward compatibility, 99.999% system availability, and public digital trust over a 10-year horizon.
